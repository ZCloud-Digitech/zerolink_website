Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Zerolink

What is Zerolink?

Zerolink is a premium studio gallery system that allows photographers to share photos with clients directly from their local computer. No cloud uploads required - clients can view and select photos instantly.

How does local hosting work?

Your photos stay on your computer. Zerolink creates a secure local server that generates shareable links. Clients access photos through their browser while files remain on your machine - 100% private and secure.

What are the pricing options?

We offer flexible plans: Monthly at ₹799, Yearly at ₹7,999 (best value - save 2 months), or Lifetime license (contact us for pricing). Choose the plan that fits your studio needs.

How do clients select photos?

Clients open the gallery link on their phone or computer. They simply tap/click a heart icon on photos they like. You receive the list of selected filenames instantly in your admin panel.

What formats are supported?

Zerolink supports all common image formats including JPG, PNG, WEBP, and displays RAW file previews. 4K resolution is fully supported for the best viewing experience.

Do I need technical knowledge?

Not at all! Setup takes just 2 minutes. Point to your photo folder, get a shareable link, and you're done. Our interface is designed for photographers, not developers.

Is there customer support?

Yes! Lifetime support is included with your license. Reach us via WhatsApp or email for any assistance. We typically respond within 24 hours.
